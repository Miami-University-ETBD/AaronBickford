Welcome to Freddy's Pizzeria!

Freddy's was founded in 1987 by William B. Afton. The first location was created in Hurricane, Utah. The small family restaurant slowly grew in popularity because of the new mascot Freddy Fazbear. The mascot was made into an animatronic for the restaurant by Edwin Martinelli. The restaurant has slowly grown all around Utah, and the animatronics have been mimicked for all the restaurants.
