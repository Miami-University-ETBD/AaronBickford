Freddy's Pizzeria

The perfect place to eat and see our world class animatronics.

Freddy's was founded in 1987 by William B. Afton. First Location was created in Hurricane, Utah. The small family restaurant slowly grew in popularity because of the new mascot Freddy Fivebear. The mascot was made into an animatronic for the restaurants by Edwin Martinelli. The restaurant has slowly grown all around Utah and the animatronics have have been mimicked for all the restaurants.
